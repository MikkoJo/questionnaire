{% blocktrans %} This softGIS questionnaire collects data about the residents’ relationship with Pacific Beach. It also collects suggestions on how the area should be developed in the future. The results will be used as a basis for discussion among all those who are interested in improving the quality of life in PB. By responding to this questionnaire you can inform the BeautifulPB-initiative and its partners about the issues that you feel are important in your neighbourhood and other parts of PB. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The survey is carried out as a cooperation of Aalto University (Helsinki, Finland) and the BeautifulPB-initiative. Aalto University has been developing the softGIS-method since 2005 in cooperation with Finnish cities interested in participatory urban planning. Recently it carried out a project with Japan and Australia, and this survey is the first time that the softGIS is tested in the US. The results of the survey will be utilized by the BeautifulPB and other interested parties to better understand how PB residents use the area and how they would like to see it developing in the future. The results may also be used by the Finnish research team to analyse the perceived environmental quality and everyday mobility issues. Marketta Kyttä and Kaisa Schmidt-Thomé (kaisa.schmidt-thome(at)aalto.fi) from Aalto University are in charge of the research. The contact persons of the Beautiful PB for the survey are Kristen Victor (kristen(at)sustainabilitymatters.us) and Sara Berns (sara(at)pacificbeach.org). {% endblocktrans %} {% trans 'Lisätietoa tutkimuksesta' %}.
